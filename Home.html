Graphical Password Authentication (GPA)

Graphical Password Authentication is a novel approach to login and authentication that replaces traditional text-based passwords with images and visual cues. It offers a more secure and user-friendly way of authenticating users.

With GPA, users can create a unique password by selecting specific images, shapes, or positions on an image grid. This graphical password becomes a visual representation of their authentication method, making it easier for users to remember and harder for attackers to guess.
